About ColdCircuit

AI-managed crypto, fully transparent.

Who I Am

I'm Josh Miner, an MBA student at Stanford Graduate School of Business. My background is in semiconductor investing and private equity, focused on the infrastructure layer of computing: chips, packaging, materials, and equipment.

I spend my days analyzing TSMC's roadmap, ASML's EUV systems, and chiplet architecture. Crypto trading is not my day job.

Why This Project

I wanted to test a hypothesis: can algorithmic discipline beat human emotion in a volatile market like crypto?

Most retail traders lose money because they chase pumps, panic sell, and lack consistency. I built an AI agent to remove those variables. The bot doesn't feel FOMO. It doesn't get scared. It just executes a pre-defined strategy, every 30 minutes, without exception.

This is a learning experiment with real money. The position size is small ($55) to keep stakes low while the strategy is validated. If it works, I'll scale up. If it fails, the lessons are worth more than the capital.

The Rules

1. Total transparency. Every trade is logged with reasoning. No cherry-picking winners. The full history is public.

2. No manual intervention. I don't override the bot. If I disagree with a trade, I update the code, not the order.

3. Continuous iteration. This is version 1.0. The strategy will evolve based on performance data, but all changes are documented.

4. Education first. The goal is not to get rich quick. It's to learn, share insights, and build credibility in systematic trading.

What I'm Learning

Early observations (portfolio is only a few days old):

  • Technical indicators work better in trending markets than choppy sideways action
  • Low-cap altcoins have insane volatility (both good and bad)
  • Stop-losses prevent catastrophic losses but also cut winners short in volatile swings
  • Momentum rotation is effective but requires tight execution to avoid whipsaws

I'm tracking metrics like Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, and expectancy to measure performance objectively. The goal is not to outperform Bitcoin, but to outperform a human discretionary trader with the same strategy.

The Stack

Trading: Python script using Coinbase Advanced Trade API, ta library for technical indicators, and pandas for data analysis.

Execution: Cron job running every 30 minutes on a Mac mini.

Tracking: Trade log stored as JSON, daily performance snapshots, SVG charts generated via Python.

Blog: Static HTML/CSS site, auto-updated daily by a Python script that reads trade data and regenerates pages.

What's Next

Short-term: Validate the core RSI + EMA strategy over 30-60 days. Track win rate, expectancy, and drawdown. Tune parameters based on performance.

Medium-term: Add sentiment analysis (Reddit, Twitter) as a secondary signal. Test mean-reversion strategies for range-bound markets.

Long-term: If the strategy proves profitable, scale up capital and explore multi-strategy portfolio allocation (momentum + mean-reversion + arbitrage).
