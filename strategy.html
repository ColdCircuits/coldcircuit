Trading Strategy

How the AI makes decisions. Technical analysis meets momentum rotation.

Overview

This portfolio is managed by an AI agent running on a 30-minute cron schedule. The bot uses a combination of technical indicators and momentum analysis to identify swing trading opportunities in liquid altcoins on Coinbase.

The strategy is transparent by design. Every trade is logged with the exact reasoning, making this a real-time case study in algorithmic trading.

Core Strategy: RSI + EMA Swing Trading

The bot uses two primary technical indicators:

  • RSI (Relative Strength Index) - 14-period RSI to identify oversold (RSI < 30) and overbought (RSI > 70) conditions
  • EMA Crossover - 12-period and 26-period exponential moving averages to confirm trend direction

Buy Signal

- RSI < 30 (oversold)
- EMA(12) crosses above EMA(26) (bullish momentum)
- Price is above EMA(26) (uptrend confirmation)

Sell Signal

- RSI > 70 (overbought)
- EMA(12) crosses below EMA(26) (bearish momentum)
- OR stop-loss triggered (-8%)
- OR take-profit triggered (+15%)

Momentum Rotation

In addition to swing trading current positions, the bot actively scans a universe of 15+ liquid altcoins for rotation opportunities. If a non-held coin shows stronger momentum than current holdings, the bot will rotate capital.

Rotation criteria:

  • 24-hour volume > $10M (liquidity threshold)
  • RSI divergence from current positions
  • Strong EMA crossover signal
  • Recent price momentum > 5%

Risk Management

Strict rules to prevent overtrading and catastrophic losses:

  • Stop-loss: Automatic sell at -8% from entry
  • Take-profit: Automatic sell at +15% from entry
  • Daily trade limit: Maximum 2 trades per day
  • Portfolio pause: If total value drops below $40, trading pauses until manual review
  • Position sizing: Equal-weight allocation across 2-3 positions (no single-asset concentration)

Why AI-Managed?

Human traders struggle with discipline, emotion, and consistency. An algorithmic approach removes these variables:

  • No FOMO - The bot doesn't chase pumps or panic sell
  • Consistent execution - Same rules applied every cycle, no exceptions
  • 24/7 monitoring - Scans markets every 30 minutes, never sleeps
  • Data-driven - Decisions based purely on technical indicators, not narratives or hype

Trade Universe

The bot actively trades or monitors these assets:

BTC, ETH, SOL, DOGE, ADA, AVAX, LINK, DOT, MATIC,
XLM, SKY, ZEC, NEAR, FET, ALGO

Selection criteria: High liquidity on Coinbase, proven volatility for swing trading, avoid memecoins without utility.

Execution

Platform: Coinbase Advanced Trade API

Schedule: Runs every 30 minutes via cron

Order type: Market orders (immediate execution, no slippage risk from limit order delays)

Code: Open-source Python script using ta library for indicators

What This Is Not

This is an experiment, not financial advice. The goal is to learn, iterate, and share lessons transparently. Small position size ($55 starting capital) means low stakes and high learning.

I am not a professional trader. I am a Stanford MBA student and semiconductor investor testing whether algorithmic discipline can outperform human discretion in crypto markets.
